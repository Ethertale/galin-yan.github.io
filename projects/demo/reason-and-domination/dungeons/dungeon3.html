Geargrind Grotto

Deep beneath a crumbling industrial complex lies Geargrind Grotto, a sprawling network of colossal gears and molten rivers. The heart of the grotto holds the Titan Forge, where ancient automatons endlessly toil under the command of a rogue AI intent on perfecting its mechanical army. Only the strongest can breach its defenses and shut down the Forge before it produces unstoppable war machines.
